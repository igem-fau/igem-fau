      17.-19.05.2019

      Erlangen, Germany

      Gather around, start working together

      iGEM, although being a competition, it lives from its big community and all the collaborations. For this reason we want to meet all of you as soon as possible and start working together! Come join us in Erlangen, Germany for a weekend of socializing, interesting talks, workshops, poster sessions and our collaboration speed-dating.

      Oh, and Franconia is known worldwide for its amazing beer.

      Speakers

      Prof. Dr. Thomas Winkler
      Immunology
      Prof. Dr. Andreas Winterpacht
      Epigenetics
      Prof. Dr. Matthias Peipp
      Bi-functional antibodies

      Location

      Erlangen
      The meetup is taking place in our lovely hometown Erlangen and is located in Bavaria, between Nuremberg and Bamberg. Around 35% of the people living here are students, so the spirit is really relaxed and we definitely have many bars and breweries you gonna love our beer! Erlangen is also known for the big amount of bicyclist, so we have really nice air and its so much fun to chill in the palace garden while having a picnic. But dont be afraid if you come here and of course cant bring your own bicycle- you can get everywhere interesting with the bus! Do you already know the Bavarian kitchen? If you havent, be prepared for tasty sausages, schuferle and dumplings!
      FAU
      FAU stands for Friedrich-Alexander university, named after the two founders. As there are 40 000 students, the FAU is the third largest university of Bavaria and offers 261 degree courses. This winter semester 2018/2019 a new elite master degree course Integrated Immunology was initiated, in which 10 members of our iGEM Team Erlangen are participating. For the FAU research and development, just as international cooperation are very fundamental principles. According to that there are 500 partnerships with universities all over the world.
